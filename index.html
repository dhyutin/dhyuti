About me

As an Artificial Intelligence Master's Grad student from Northwestern University with a strong interest in Machine Learning, Computer Vision and NLP, I have honed my skills through various internships in these areas, as well as in Backend WebDev, Statistical Data Analytics, Graph Theory and User Interface Design. Proficient in C/C++ and Python, I am well-versed in a variety of programming tools and frameworks such as cv2, pandas, matplotlib, tensorflow, keras, nltk, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. I am also experienced in parallelizing complex ML algorithms with openMP and CUDA, and am comfortable working with a variety of operating systems

In addition to my technical qualifications, I have also gained valuable leadership experience through my involvement in my institute's Alumni Affairs student team, and through my role as Core for my Institute's Music Club and Secretary for Vidhai, an NPO affiliated to IIITDM Kancheepuram. These experiences have helped me to develop strong people skills, which I believe are essential to pursuing my career aspirations.

As I actively seek internships and opportunities in the fields of AI, I am open to discussing new ideas and projects, and welcome connections around the world.
