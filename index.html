About me

As a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence at Northwestern University, with a strong focus on Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Natural Language Processing (NLP), I have developed my skills through various internships in these fields, as well as in Backend Web Development, Statistical Data Analytics, Graph Theory, and User Interface Design. Proficient in C/C++ and Python, I am well-versed in a range of programming tools and frameworks such as OpenCV (cv2), pandas, matplotlib, TensorFlow, Keras, NLTK, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. Additionally, I have experience parallelizing complex ML algorithms using OpenMP and CUDA, and I am comfortable working across different operating systems.

Beyond my technical expertise, I have gained valuable leadership experience through my involvement in my institute’s Alumni Affairs student team, and in roles such as Core Member of the Institute’s Music Club and Secretary for Vidhai, an NPO affiliated with IIITDM Kancheepuram. These roles have helped me hone my interpersonal and leadership skills, which I believe are essential for achieving my career goals.
